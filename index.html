The dynamics of Nipah virus (NiV) transmission and analysis

Introduction: In this project, we formulated mathematical model to understand the transmission dynamics of Nipah Virus from Pigs to humans and from humans to humans. The model focused on the effect of combined strategies vaccine and the use of condom in reducing the spread of Nipah virus. Python programing was used for the simulation and visualization. The model gives an insight on how vaccination and condom use can help and control the spread of the NIV especially among the Nipah Virus Carriers (asymptomatic carriers) who are the amplified infectious people. When there are combine strategy of vaccine and condom use, the reproduction number is less than one, thereby reducing the spread of NiV.

    Skills and Tools Used:

  1. Programming Language: Python.    DOWNLOAD the Published Article
    2. and   VIEW the simulation
  2. Mathematical Tools: Ordinary Differential Equations (ODEs), Central Manifold Theory
  3. Libraries for Analysis and Simulation:
    • SciPy: For numerical integration and solving ODEs.
    • Matplotlib : For visualizing simulation results.
    • NumPy: For array handling and numerical operations.

Insight: Vaccination and condom use are very effective management strategies for reducing the spread of NiV. These strategies help reduce the reproduction number below 1. Asymptomatic carriers of NiV are the most infectious individuals, and targeting this group with vaccination and other prevention strategies could be an effective approach to reduce the spread of NiV. The model confirms that the disease-free equilibrium is globally stable, while the endemic equilibrium is locally asymptotically stable when the reproduction number is near unity.
